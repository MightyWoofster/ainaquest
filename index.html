ʻĀinaQuest

Hot-seat prototype (2–6 players) • 3 rounds • Plant → Huli! → Pass

Set up players

Enter 2–6 player names. This version is “pass-the-device.”

What’s implemented?
  • 3 rounds. Deal 9 cards per player each round.
  • Each turn: each player secretly chooses 1 card to plant face-down. Then Huli! reveals all planted cards and hands get passed.
  • End of round scoring: sums points for non-invasive, non-resource cards. Invasives are tracked across rounds and used for the -20 rule at the end.
  • Resource effects (Wai, Pākukui) and set bonuses are stubbed for later.
